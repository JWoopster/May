May Poem

May, the month of blooms and light,

I'd love to share with you its sight,

Together we could stroll and play,

Enjoying the warmth of a spring day.


May, your presence is what I seek,

To walk with you and hear you speak,

In nature's beauty we'll bask,

And from the world's noise, we'll unmask.


So May, would you do me the honor,

And join me for a date to savor,

No pressure, no need to rush,

Just a chance for two hearts to crush.
